# Hack Night

Join [Cascade STEAM](/) for [Hack Night](/hack-night) on Saturdays from 4pm-"Late" at the Bellingham Makerspace for an open, collaborative session of shared learning! Bring your projects, homework, questions, and ideas and let’s do them together.

P.S. If you are looking for project ideas, then we’ve got them! See our [Digital Navigators](/digital-navigators), [HelpDesk](/helpdesk), and Stormwater Monitoring [Community Projects](/community-projects) for inspiration.

Check the [Cascade STEAM Community Hub](http://hub.cascadesteam.org) or the [Cascade STEAM Meetup Page](https://meetup.com/cascadesteam) for details and to RSVP for all scheduled events.
